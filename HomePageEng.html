THE BLOCKCHAIN

The Blockchain is a shared and immutable ledger in chronological order. The information is organized in fixed-size blocks, where each ledger entry corresponds to a block. Each block includes an increasing date and time (hours, minutes, seconds), ensuring an accurate timeline of the records within the blockchain.
